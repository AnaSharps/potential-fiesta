BingeBox

BingeBox is a Chrome extension for watching Prime Video & Netflix remotely with friends , e.g., for movie nights with that long-distance special someone. It synchronizes video playback and adds group voice call and chat. Gives the feel of theatre.

A new way to watch at home together

Binge Box is a new way to watch Prime Videos and Netflix with your friends online.
Binge Box synchronizes video playback and adds group calls and chat to your favorite shows.
Join over people and use Binge Box to link up with friends and host long distance movie nights and TV watch parties today!

Features

Sync Movies & Shows in HD

Always stay precisely in sync when you're watching shows together. Enjoy HD video quality and fast buffering in all of your Binge parties.

Instant Connect

Create parties in seconds right on the Netflix.com website to watch thousands of great Netflix shows together with friends.

Group Voice call

While the video is playing, users can socialize with other participants through a built-in chat feature that supports both call and text with built-in emojis.

How it works

  1. Install Binge Box
  2. Open a Prime Video or Netflix video
  3. Create a Binge party
  4. Join a Binge Box party
